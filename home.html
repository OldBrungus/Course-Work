Restaurant Description

Piggo Bistro is Glasgow's premier American dining experience, bringing the flavors of the United States to the historic Gallowgate. Providing an atmosphere of humble charm, our diners will be transported accross the Atlantic to the American heartland. Whether stopping in on your lunch break, treating the family to a peek into american culture, or refueling after a thrilling match at Paradise, Piggo Bistro has something for everyone. Although our dining area artfully captures the aesthetic of American casual, our chefs are world class and ready to surprise you with their masterful presentations of American classics.
